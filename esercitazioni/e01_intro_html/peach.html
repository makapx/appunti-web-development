Peach

Peach is a fictional character in the Mario video game franchise, owned by Nintendo and created by Japanese video game designer Shigeru Miyamoto. She is the princess of the fictional Mushroom Kingdom, which is constantly under attack by Bowser. Peach first appeared in Super Mario Bros., and has appeared in many Mario spin-off games, such as Mario Party, Mario Kart, and Super Smash Bros. She has also appeared in many sports games, such as Mario Tennis and Mario Golf. Peach is portrayed as the damsel in distress in most of the Mario games. Her main goal is to find Mario and rescue him from Bowser. She is the princess of the Mushroom Kingdom. She is the girlfriend of Mario and the mother of Pauline, who is also known as Lady in the games.
